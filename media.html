Research Impact and Media Coverage

Some of my research and work appeared in the media.

Boston Schools Transportation
  • The Wall Street Journal, How Do You Fix a School-Bus Problem? Call MIT, Aug 11, 2017.
  • The Boston Globe, Boston school bus performance improves dramatically, Sept 08, 2017.
  • WBUR radio, 2 MIT Engineers Use Math To Plot A Path For Boston's School Buses (in-depth article), Jul 27, 2017.
  • Siam News, A School Bus Trip to the Crossroads of Policy and Optimization (research blog post), Nov 21, 2017.
  • This project also appeared on multiple news websites, local TV and radio news channels.
Schools Start Times Choice
  • The Boston Globe, The Equity Machine. An in-depth study of our start time project and its consequences in Boston. Sept 20, 2018.
  • Wired, What the Boston School Bus Schedule Can Teach Us About AI. A reflection by Joichi Ito on the complexity of the interactions between algorithms and policy, using the example of my project. Nov. 5, 2018.
  • Rhode Island State House Start Time Commission, Video of our presentation on the complexity of school start time choices and potential solutions, Mar 19, 2018.
  • Boston School Committee, The Boston School Committee voted that school start times should be chosen using precise and analytically verifiable criteria, using our research as a basis for their decisions, Dec 09, 2017.
